## Compliance / Claim Guardrails

Important official sources found:

- FSSAI Health Supplements page: standards for health supplements and nutraceuticals are under Food Safety and Standards regulations; categories include health supplements, nutraceuticals, FSDU, FSMP, specialty food containing botanicals, probiotics, prebiotics, and novel foods.
- FSSAI Advertising and Claims Regulations: health claims need substantiation and must include physiological role plus product composition relevance; product-attributed health benefits require stronger scientific evidence.
- FSSAI Ayurveda Aahara Regulations, 2022: Ayurveda Aahara excludes Ayurvedic drugs/proprietary Ayurvedic medicines, cosmetics, narcotic/psychotropic substances, certain herbs/metals, etc.
- Labelling snippets from FSSAI consultation include front/back pack requirements for health supplement/nutraceutical categories such as "NOT FOR MEDICINAL USE", recommended usage level, duration, warnings, not a substitute for varied diet, keep out of reach of children, etc.

Sources:
- https://fssai.gov.in/cms/health-supplements.php
- https://www.fssai.gov.in/upload/uploadfiles/files/Compendium_Advertising_Claims_Regulations_04_03_2021.pdf
- https://www.fssai.gov.in/upload/notifications/2022/05/62789a20b54bdGazette_Notification_Ayurveda_Aahara_09_05_2022.pdf
- https://fssai-digest.fssai.gov.in/perspectives-and-dialogues/ayurveda-aahara.php
- https://comments.fssai.gov.in/Bestviewwl.aspx?NOTIFICATION_ID=4121

Practical guardrails for Nirogidhara website:

- Avoid absolute medical promises: "cures obesity", "guaranteed fat loss", "burns fat fast", "permanent weight loss".
- Prefer softer support language: "supports metabolism", "supports healthy weight-management routine", "helps complement diet and activity".
- Add disclaimers: "Not for medicinal use", "Not a substitute for a varied diet", "Results vary", "Consult a physician if pregnant, nursing, under medication, or managing a medical condition."
- Publish license/certification details only after verifying them from documents.
- Show full ingredients, dosage, contraindications, manufacturer, batch/expiry, and customer support.
